baddie

noun
it-girl stunner slay queen
basic wallflower plain jane

A confident, stylish, and attractive woman. Unapologetically bold in their style and attitude.

She walked into the room like a total baddie. Everyone stopped and stared.

"Baddie" blew up through Black American slang and spread via Instagram and TikTok around 2015–2020. You'll hear it in rap lyrics, beauty communities, and gen z internet culture.

From AAVE where "bad" means impressive/attractive (the opposite of its literal meaning). "Baddie" is the noun form — the "-ie" suffix making it casual and affectionate.
